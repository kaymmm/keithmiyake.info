Researcher – June 17th Readings – Coolies

I thought it was interesting to find a coolie contract. We understand coolie labor as similar to slave labor, with the exception that they are paid (much less than a regular worker) and looking at a contract like this, I wonder how many workers actually understood what they were signing and whether they had any legal recourse should their contracts be violated or broken.

Kristina Szeto
