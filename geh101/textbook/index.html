Textbook

Keith Miyake •  Permalink

The textbook that will be used this semester is Introduction to Geography, Twelfth Edition, by Getis, Getis, and Fellmann. It contains all of the readings for the course and is being used across all of the sections of this course being taught this semester.

The bibliography entry for the book is as follows:

Getis, Arthur, Judith Getis, and Jerome D. Fellmann. Introduction to Geography. 12 ed. New York: McGraw-Hill, 2009. ISBN: 978-0-07352282-1.

A thirteenth (13th) edition was recently published, but it is likely to be more expensive than the older, 12th edition. Earlier editions are available, and you are welcome to use what you can find, but I would recommend against anything earlier than the 9th edition.
