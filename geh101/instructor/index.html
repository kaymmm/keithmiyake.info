Instructor

Keith Miyake •  Permalink

Keith K. Miyake is a doctoral student at the City University of New York (CUNY) Graduate Center. He has been a Graduate Teaching Fellow in the Lehman College Environmental, Geographic, and Geologic Sciences (EGGS) Department since fall 2010.

Keith’s research interests include political ecology, environmental justice, and environmental governance, critical race theory, feminist and queer theory, radical cartography, militant co-research and radical pedagogy.
